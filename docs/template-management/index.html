Template Management

Learn how to create, save, export, import, and share your custom naming schemas.

Saving & Managing Templates

FileNamer saves all of your custom naming templates locally in your browser's persistent database. You can manage your schemas using the controls inside the template bar.

Create Templates

To create a new, custom naming template, click the Add Template button in the template header. You can then add static segments or dynamic fields like dates, text inputs, and counters.

Export & Import Templates

You can backup your configurations or move them to another computer by clicking the Export button. This saves your templates as a structured .json configuration file. To load them back, click Import and select the configuration file.

Share Templates

If you want to share a specific template layout with your team, you can generate a Shareable URL. Click the share button to copy a direct link to your configuration details directly to your clipboard.
